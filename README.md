# 🌍 Air Quality Index (AQI) Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze air quality across different cities. It focuses on key pollutants such as CO, NO, SO2, and PM2.5 to monitor environmental conditions and support data-driven decision-making. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}  

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Monitor air pollution levels across cities
- Analyze AQI trends over time
- Identify highly polluted and cleaner regions
- Support environmental and policy decision-making

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Key KPIs
- Average CO (Carbon Monoxide)
- Average NO (Nitrogen Oxides)
- Average SO2 (Sulfur Dioxide)
- Average PM2.5

### 🔹 Visualizations
- Line Chart → AQI trends over time
- Bar Chart → Top 5 polluted cities
- Bar Chart → Least polluted cities

### 🔹 Interactivity
- Slicers (City, Year)
- Dynamic filtering and cross-visual interactions

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Identified cities with high pollution levels
- Observed trends and fluctuations in AQI over time
- PM2.5 emerged as a major contributor to poor air quality

---

## 🚀 Business / Policy Recommendations
- Implement stricter emission controls in high-pollution cities
- Improve public transport to reduce CO and NO levels
- Promote renewable energy to lower SO2 emissions
